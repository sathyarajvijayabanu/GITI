About us

Welcome to GOVT ITI

Welcome To Govt.Industrial Training Institute (Tribal), Kollihills.

kollihills is a taluk and located in Namakkal district in the state of Tamil Nadu, India. It is located 54 km from Namakkal.

Kolli Hills or Kolli Malai is a small mountain range located in Central Tamil Nadu and spread over the Namakkal district and Tiruchirapalli district of India.

The mountains rise to approximately 1500 m[1] in height and cover an area of approximately 280 km2.

They are part of the Eastern Ghats, which is a mountain range that runs mostly parallel to the east coast of Southern India, Kollihills Govt ITI is a newly formed among 5 ST ITI’s from Tamilnadu . ITI Functioning from 2013.
