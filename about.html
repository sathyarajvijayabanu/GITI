Welcome to GOVT ITI

Welcome To Govt.Industrial Training Institute (Tribal), Kollihills.

kollihills is a taluk and located in Namakkal district in the state of Tamil Nadu, India. It is located 54 km from Namakkal.

Kolli Hills or Kolli Malai is a small mountain range located in Central Tamil Nadu and spread over the Namakkal district and Tiruchirapalli district of India.

The mountains rise to approximately 1500 m[1] in height and cover an area of approximately 280 km2.

They are part of the Eastern Ghats, which is a mountain range that runs mostly parallel to the east coast of Southern India, Kollihills Govt ITI is a newly formed among 5 ST ITI’s from Tamilnadu . ITI Functioning from 2013.

Our Mission

Educating students for career success. We're here for you. Our Job is to make sure your ITI experience is everything it should be exciting, stimulating and successful.

We will be a full opportunity with high quality programs of teaching, training and public service.

These programs will provide our students and community with the knowledge, Skills, Understanding and values required to empower them to create a better world.

03

Our vission

The primary purpose of the GOVT Industrial Training Institute, Kollihills is to provide a learning environment in which faculty, staff and students can discover, examine critically, preserve and transmit the knowledge, wisdom and values that will help ensure the survival of this and future generations and improve the quality of life for all.

The ITI seeks to help students to develop an understanding and appreciation for the complex cultural and physical worlds in which they live and to realize their highest potential of intellectual, physical and human development.
